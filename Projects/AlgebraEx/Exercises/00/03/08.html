We infer that $c^2 \equiv 0 \pmod{4}$, namely, $c^2$ is divisible by 4, we also see that $a^2+b^2$ is divisible by 4 but more importantly, from 0.3.7, we infer that $a^2$ and $b^2$ are each divisible by 4.
