Preliminaries: #0.3.15

For simplicity, we use the program we made to find the gcd of 2 numbers as well as their combination.

  1. $\gcd{(20,13)} = 1$, $(2)20 +(-3)13 = 1$ so $-3 \equiv 17 \pmod{20}$ is the multiplicative inverse.
  2. $\gcd{(89,69)} = 1$, $(-31)89 +(40)69 = 1$ so 40 is the multiplicative inverse.
  3. $\gcd{(3797,1891)} = 1$, $(-126)3797 +(253)1891 = 1$ so 253 is the multiplicative inverse.
  4. $\gcd{(77695236973,6003722857)} = 1$, $(17)20 +(-220)13 = 1$ so $-220 \equiv 77695236753 \pmod{77695236973}$ is the multiplicative inverse.
