Chapter 1: #1.3.3

Using the disjoint cycle decomposition, we can compute the order easily: it is the least common multiple of the lengths of each cycle. \begin{align*} |\sigma| &= \text{lcm}(4,3,6) = 12\\ |\tau| &= \text{lcm}(2,5,2,3,2) = 30\\ |\sigma^2| &= \text{lcm}(2,3,3,3,2) = 6\\ |\sigma\tau| &= \text{lcm}(3,2,6,2)\\ |\tau\sigma| &= \text{lcm}(2,2,6,3) = 6\\ |\tau^2\sigma| &=\text{lcm}(13) = 13 \end{align*}
