Chapter 1: #1.7.5

Denote $\ker(\varphi)$ to be the kernel of the permutation representation and $K$ be the kernel of the action. We have $\ker(\varphi) \subseteq K$ because if $\varphi(g) = \text{id}$, then $\forall a \in A, ga = a$ and we have $\ker(\varphi) \supseteq K$ because if $\forall a \in A, ga = a$, then $\varphi(g) = \text{id}$.
