Chapter 1: #1.1.4

We have seen that $\bar{a} \cdot \bar{b} = \overline{a\cdot b}$ so we have the following: \begin{align*} \left(\bar{a}\cdot \bar{b}\right) \cdot \bar{c} &= \overline{a\cdot b} \cdot \bar{c}\\ &= \overline{a\cdot b\cdot c}\\ &= \bar{a} \cdot \overline{b\cdot c}\\ &= \bar{a} \cdot \left(\bar{b}\cdot \bar{c}\right) \end{align*}
