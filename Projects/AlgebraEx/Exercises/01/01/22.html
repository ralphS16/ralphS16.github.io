Chapter 1: #1.1.22

Denote $n = |x|$ and $m = |g^{-1}xg|$, since $x^n = 1$, we get $$\left(g^{-1}xg\right)^n = g^{-1}xgg^{-1}xg\cdots g^{-1}xg = g^{-1}x^ng = g^{-1}g = 1$$ so it follows that $m \leq n$. Also, since $\left(g^{-1}xg\right)^m = g^{-1}x^mg = 1$, we have $x^m = gg^{-1}x^mgg^{-1} = gg^{-1} = 1$ so $n \leq m$ and we are done.
